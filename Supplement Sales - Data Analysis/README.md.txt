# Supplement Sales Data Analysis

## Overview
This project analyses weekly sales data for nutritional supplements to uncover meaningful business insights. By examining revenue trends, product categories, seasonal purchasing patterns, and the impact of discounts and returns, the analysis aims to guide strategic decision-making and highlight opportunities for growth.

## Features
- **Data Import & Cleaning**: Load and preprocess weekly sales data, including handling dates, missing values, and returns.
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**:
  - Sales and revenue trends over time.
  - Contribution of categories and products to overall revenue.
  - Seasonal and cyclical patterns in customer purchasing behaviour.
  - Impact of discounts and product returns on net revenue.
- **Visualisations**:
  - **Time Series Analysis**: Weekly and monthly revenue and units sold.
  - **Category Performance**: Net revenue by product category.
  - **Discounts & Returns**: Effect on revenue and customer satisfaction.
  - **Customer Insights**: Patterns in product demand across different periods.

## Tools Used
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)**: Data analysis and interactive visualisation.
- **Jupyter Notebook**: For documenting and executing the analysis.
- **CSV**: Data source format.

## Key Insights
- Identified top-performing supplement categories and products contributing to revenue.
- Detected seasonal trends in supplement sales, valuable for inventory planning.
- Measured the effect of discounts and returns on overall profitability.
- Highlighted opportunities to optimise pricing and promotional strategies.
